# Evice Labs

**The frontier of confidential compute and cryptographic execution.**

Evice Labs is an independent, open collective of builders, cryptographers, and protocol architects. We are dedicated to advancing the Logos ecosystem through the design and implementation of zero-knowledge proofs (zkVM), sBPF confidential state systems, and robust on-chain verification mechanisms.

### Our Philosophy

We believe in building with precision. We are intentionally small, prioritizing deep expertise and high-conviction engineering over rapid expansion. Evice Labs serves as a vehicle for independent builders to collaborate on hard, meaningful problems while retaining their individual track records.
